About Helios

Helios is a specialized geographic analysis platform engineered to identify high-value solar energy opportunities within Toronto’s urban landscape.

By processing the surface dimensions in the parking lots operated by Green P along with its solar irradiance potential, we provide actionable financial insights into installation feasibility, revenue generation, and long-term sustainability of implementing solar energy panels on these places.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between underutilized urban space and clean energy integration, creating a business opportunity for Green P, where the use of solar panels on their facilities could signify an additional energy source capable of powering EV charging stations, making an innovative source of income.
